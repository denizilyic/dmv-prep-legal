## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I start the 3-day free trial?

Open the app → tap any locked feature (Mock Exam or Study Topics)
→ tap **Start 3-day free trial** on the Monthly plan. Apple will
ask you to confirm with your Apple ID. You won't be charged until
day 3 — cancel any time before then and pay nothing.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions** on your iPhone
or iPad → tap **Florida DMV — Your Language** → **Cancel
Subscription**. Apple controls all subscription billing — we don't
have a separate cancel flow inside the app.

If you cancel during the 3-day trial, you won't be charged.

### I bought Lifetime and didn't pass the FL DMV exam first try. How do I get a refund?

Open the **Apple Support** app on your iPhone or visit
[reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com),
sign in with your Apple ID, find the Florida DMV — Your Language
purchase, and request a refund within 30 days of your test date.

We'll back any reasonable refund request — Apple handles the actual
processing.

### I switched to a new iPhone. How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → **Settings** → **Restore purchases**. As long as
you're signed in to the same Apple ID, your subscription or Lifetime
purchase will reactivate automatically.

### My native language isn't listed.

Right now we support **English, Spanish, Haitian Creole, Portuguese,
and Turkish**. We're adding more — Vietnamese and Mandarin are
next on the list. If your language isn't here yet, email us and
we'll bump it up the queue if there's demand.

### I found a question with a wrong answer / typo / bad translation.

Tap the **🚩 flag icon** at the top of the question screen, choose
the issue type, and submit. The report goes straight to our
question-quality queue — no account needed. We review every flag
within a few days.

### Does this app work on iPad?

Yes. The app runs full-screen on any iPad and uses the same
account/subscription as iPhone.

### Does my progress sync between devices?

Not yet — progress is stored locally on each device. iCloud sync
is on the roadmap for v1.1. Your subscription/Lifetime entitlement
DOES sync (handled by Apple) — only the per-device study history
is local for now.

### Is this an official Florida DMV app?

No. We're not affiliated with the Florida Department of Highway
Safety and Motor Vehicles (FLHSMV). The official Florida Driver
License Handbook is free at [flhsmv.gov](https://flhsmv.gov). We
build a study tool around it.

### How accurate are your practice questions?

Questions are written based on patterns observed by drivers who
actually took the FL DMV Class E Knowledge Exam. We update the
question bank regularly as new exam patterns are reported. We
don't reproduce the official exam — we teach the same material in
a way that gets you ready for it.

### What happens if I fail the FL DMV exam?

You can retake the exam at any DMV office. Florida lets you retest
multiple times. If you bought Lifetime and didn't pass the first
time, you're eligible for a refund (see refund FAQ above).
